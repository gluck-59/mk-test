652 AUDIO & GAUGES Boom!™ Audio – ’14-’16 Touring A. BOOM!™ AUDIO AMPLIFIER KIT Take your music to the streets. Boom!™ Audio Amplifier Kits allow you to crank up the volume with significantly less distortion. The amplifier provides more power than Original Equipment radio alone, resulting in increased volume, fuller sound reproduction, reduced clipping and lower Total Harmonic Distortion (THD) than head unit alone. Unlike after- market amplifiers that connect between the radio and the speakers, the Boom! Audio amplifier bypasses the radio’s internal power amp to produce the cleanest sound possi- ble. The Boom! Audio Amplifier boosts the pure source signal, minimizing any distortion generated through the speaker output. The system-matched high-output signal features optimized sound equalization for crisp, clear vocals and maximum bass response and the strong midrange response cuts through wind and road noise. The waterproof 4-chan- nel, 75-watts/channel amplifier (300-watt total) has been designed from the ground-up for the harsh motorcycle envi- ronment.
