The Screamin’ Eagle family of EFI Tuners provide the ® computer-based tools you need to record and edit the ECM tuning tables for fuel delivery and spark timing. The Tuning mode allows you to alter speciﬁ c variable in your EFI system, and the Data mode enables you to record and display real-time vehicle data and graph the results on screen. The Tuner software also includes a variety of pre-tuned calibrations that can be saved and loaded into your ECM.
