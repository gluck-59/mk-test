H-D DETACHABLES™ LOCKING LEATHER ® SADDLEBAGS These large-capacity lockable detachable saddlebags are completely removable with the simple pull of the internal handle, leaving your bike clean and ready to cruise the boule- vard. The handsome leather slant-style bags feature hidden quick-release clips behind the classic valise straps and black buckles. When removed, the large capacity bags leave behind two simple, inconspicuous docking points. The bags are ® compatible with both rigid and H-D Detachables™ accesso- ries when installed with the appropriate docking hardware kits.
