Boom!™ Audio Amplifier Kits allow you to crank up the volume with significantly less distortion. The amplifier provides more power than Original Equipment radio alone, resulting in increased volume, fuller sound reproduction, reduced clipping and lower Total Harmonic Distortion (THD) than head unit alone. When teamed with Boom! Audio Speakers, the Boom! Amplifier provides 50% louder (6dB) system output than other popular aftermarket systems at the same volume setting. The system- matched high-output signal features optimized sound equal- ization for crisp, clear vocals and maximum bass response and the strong midrange response cuts through wind and road noise.
