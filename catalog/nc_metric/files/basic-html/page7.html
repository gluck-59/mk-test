Introduction to VStream ® N20014 Pretreated with National Cycle’s Aeroacoustic Windscreens Comes with new vent pre-installed National Cycle’s innovative VStream® Windscreen gets its name from the unique shape and dimensional contours designed and engineered into the windshield. The patented “V” profile helps push the turbulent wind out and away from the side of the rider’s head, produc- ing a quieter and more comfortable riding experience for both the rider and the passenger. VStream Windscreens are constructed from thick, durable polycarbonate and are coated with our exclu- sive Quantum™ hardcoat or FMR hardcoat (depending on model application). Fairing-Mount VStream Windscreens replace the stock windscreen and use existing OEM mounting hardware. All VStream Windscreens are manufactured in the USA and are covered by our 3-Year Warranty against breakage. ® VStream Windscreens N20012A (with vent hole; vent not included) for GL1800 Gold Wing ® VStream Windscreens for both GL1800 Gold Wing and GL1500 VStream® Special Edition: This is the ultimate windscreen Gold Wing retain complete for your GL1800!
